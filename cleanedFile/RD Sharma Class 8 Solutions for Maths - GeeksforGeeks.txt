Chapter 1: Rational Numbers The chapter Rational numbers deal with the basic concepts of Rational Numbers and their properties of mathematical operations applied to it. In this book, there is a total of six exercises based on this chapter. Exercise 1.1 and 1.2 covers the basic concepts and properties used while the problems in Exercises 1.3 and 1.4 covers the topic like simplification of expressions involving addition and subtraction of rational numbers, multiplication of rational numbers and its properties, division of rational numbers. However, the rest two exercises 1.5 and 1.6 discussed the questions based on the representation of rational numbers on the number line and rational numbers between two rational numbers.
